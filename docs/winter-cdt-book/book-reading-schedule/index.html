Book Tour: 202X Schedule

Join me for readings and discussions about "A Wild Calling" - my book about my 2022-2023 winter Continental Divide ski traverse. These events combine storytelling with practical insights about ski traversing, gear systems, and wilderness philosophy.

1. Further dates

Would you like to offer your community an evening of gripping tales and measured discussion? I'm available for additional readings, workshops, or speaking engagements. Contact me to discuss bringing this presentation to your group, outdoor club, or community.

2. Past dates and places

2.1. Leadville, CO: 2025 SoBo Trail Daze

Friday, September 12, 2025

The Continental Divide Trail Coalition's annual gathering celebrated southbound hikers. I shared stories from the winter traverse and discussed the unique challenges of ski touring the CDT.

2.2. Lima, MT: Book reading

Saturday, September 27, 2025

At the home of the Lima Bears I talked about some key moments and numbers.

2.3. Salt Lake City, UT: Skimo Co reading

Tuesday, September 30, 2025 at Chappell Brewing

The Skimo Co community showed up, and pelted me with good questions.
